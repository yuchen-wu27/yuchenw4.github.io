Fish

Information

Although fish are a popular beginner pet, they still require proper care, clean water, and a suitable environment. Learning before bringing one home is very important.

Fish Essentials

Check Supplies Notes
Fish Tank Choose the right size based on species and number of fish.
Fish Food Use food suitable for the type of fish.
Decoration Add plants or hiding places that fit their behavior.
Air Pump Select one that matches the tank size and capacity.
Heater Maintain the proper water temperature if needed.
