Checklist Before Getting a Pet

Before you bring a pet home, please give yourself 3 minutes.

  • ◻︎Can you handle shedding or fur care?
  • ◻︎How much time can you spend with them every day?
  • ◻︎Can you walk your dog 2–3 times a day? (If you choose a dog)
  • ◻︎Do you know their basic diet and how much they should eat?
  • ◻︎Are you prepared for the long-term responsibility (5–30 years)? (Different pets have different lifespans, but they all need a forever home)
  • ◻︎Can you afford routine care and general maintenance?
  • ◻︎Can you love them like your own family?
  • ◻︎Are you willing to learn about their behavior and needs?
  • ◻︎Can you accept shedding, odors, noise, scratching, biting, or damaged furniture?
  • ◻︎If they grow old or become sick, will you still stay by their side?

A pet’s life is short, but their love is infinite. Make sure you’re ready.
